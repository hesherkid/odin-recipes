Korean Beef & Broccoli

About the dish

This recipe is great for a week day meal with minimal prep and quick cook times. Once you make this dish it will become a go to for those busy weeks.

The first time I made this for my husband, we had it 2 more times that week. The amount of flavour you get from the minimal ingredients will leave you wow'd.

Ingredients:

Serving Size: 4

  • Ground Beef or Pork: 1 lb
  • Garlic: 3 cloves
  • Brown Sugar: 1/4 cup
  • Soy Sauce: 1/4 cup
  • Sesame Oil: 2 tsp
  • Ground Ginger: 1/4 tsp
  • Red Pepper Flakes: 1/4 tsp
  • Pepper: 1/4 tsp
  • Cooked White Rice: 2 cups
  • Broccoli: 1 head
  • For Garnish: Sliced Green onion, sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Wash and steam Broccoli until just about done and set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Once hot and 1/2 tbsp oil then ground beef and garlic. Cook until no pink remains, breaking into smaller pieces. 5-7 min.
  3. In a small bowl whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, red pepper flakes, and pepper.
  4. Once the beef is cooked pour the sauce over it and simmer for 2 minutes. Stir in broccoli and let cook for another minute. Remove from heat.
  5. To serve, scoop rice into bowl, top with beef and broccoli, garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
